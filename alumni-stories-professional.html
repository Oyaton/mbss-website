Excellence in Professional Fields

Our alumni have distinguished themselves across various professional sectors, demonstrating the quality education and character formation they received at MBSS. From business leadership to public service, they continue to make significant contributions to society.

Success Stories

Dr. Sarah Akello

Chief Medical Officer, Adjumani General Hospital

Class of 2008 - Dr. Akello graduated from MBSS with exceptional grades in sciences. She pursued medicine at Makerere University and later specialized in pediatrics. Today, she leads the medical team at Adjumani General Hospital and has implemented several community health programs.

"MBSS gave me the foundation in sciences and the discipline I needed to excel in medicine. The values of service and excellence instilled here continue to guide my practice."

Healthcare Leadership Community Service Medical Excellence

Eng. James Okello

Senior Software Engineer, Microsoft East Africa

Class of 2010 - James excelled in mathematics and physics at MBSS before pursuing computer engineering at Makerere University. He later earned a master's degree in the USA and now leads software development projects for Microsoft's East African operations.

"The problem-solving skills I developed at MBSS, especially in mathematics, laid the groundwork for my career in technology. The school taught me to think critically and never give up."

Technology Innovation International Career STEM Leadership

Hon. Grace Amoding

Member of Parliament, Adjumani District

Class of 2005 - Grace was an active debater and student leader at MBSS. She studied law at Uganda Christian University and has served in various leadership roles before being elected to Parliament. She champions education and women's rights.

"MBSS shaped my leadership skills through debate society and student government. The confidence I gained here prepared me for public service and advocacy."

Political Leadership Public Service Advocacy
