Pursuing Academic Excellence

MBSS alumni have continued their educational journey at prestigious universities both locally and internationally. Their academic achievements demonstrate the strong foundation they received at our school and their commitment to lifelong learning and intellectual growth.

Academic Achievements

Dr. Catherine Aluma

PhD in Environmental Science, University of Cambridge

Class of 2009 - Catherine earned her Bachelor's degree from Makerere University, Master's from University of Cape Town, and PhD from Cambridge University. She now leads climate research projects across East Africa and has published over 20 research papers.

Professor of Economics, Harvard University
