Admissions

Join Our School Community

Thank you for your interest in Monsignor Bala Secondary School. We welcome applications from students who are eager to learn, grow, and contribute positively to our school community.

Our admissions process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our academic environment and benefit from our holistic approach to education. We consider academic potential, character, and extracurricular interests when reviewing applications.

Applications for the 2025 academic year are now open. We encourage you to apply early as spaces are limited.

Admissions Process

1. Submit Application

Complete the application form and submit it along with the required documents. You can download the application form from our website or collect it from the school's admissions office.

2. Entrance Examination

Qualified applicants will be invited to take an entrance examination that assesses their knowledge and skills in key subject areas.

3. Interview

Selected candidates will be invited for an interview with members of our admissions committee. This gives us an opportunity to get to know the applicant better and for the applicant to learn more about our school.

4. Admission Decision

Admission decisions are made based on the application, entrance examination results, and interview. Successful applicants will receive an offer letter.

5. Acceptance and Enrollment

To secure your place, you must accept the offer by paying the required fees and completing the enrollment process.

Required Documents

Please submit the following documents along with your application:

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate or passport
  • Recent passport-sized photographs (4)
  • Academic records from previous school
  • Recommendation letter from previous school
  • Medical form completed by a licensed physician

All documents must be submitted in English or accompanied by certified translations.
