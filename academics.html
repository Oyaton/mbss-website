Academics

Academic Excellence

At Monsignor Bala Secondary School, we are committed to providing a comprehensive and rigorous academic program that prepares students for success in higher education and beyond. Our curriculum is designed to develop critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a lifelong love of learning.

Our dedicated teachers employ innovative teaching methods to engage students and cater to different learning styles. We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention and support for each student.

The school follows the national curriculum while incorporating additional enrichment activities to provide a well-rounded education.

Subjects Offered

We offer comprehensive educational subjects tailored to meet the diverse needs and aspirations of our students.

Ordinary Level (O-Level)

Secondary 1 to Secondary 4

Our O-Level program provides a strong foundation in core subjects while allowing students to explore their interests through elective courses.

Core Subjects:
  • Physics (PHY)
  • Biology (BIO)
  • Chemistry (CHEM)
  • Mathematics (MTC)
  • History (HIST)
  • Geography (GEO)
  • Christian Religious Education (CRE)
  • English Language (ENG)
  • Kiswahili
  • Entrepreneurship (ENT)
  • Agriculture (AGRIC)
  • Information & Communication Technology (ICT)
Learn More

Advanced Level (A-Level)

Secondary 5 to Secondary 6

Our A-Level program offers specialized subject combinations to prepare students for university education and future careers.

Science Combinations:
  • PCM: Physics (PHY), Chemistry (CHEM), Mathematics (MTC)
  • PCB: Physics (PHY), Chemistry (CHEM), Biology (BIO)
  • BCM: Biology (BIO), Chemistry (CHEM), Mathematics (MTC)
Arts Combinations:
  • HEG: History (HIST), Economics, Geography (GEO)
  • HEL: History (HIST), Economics, Literature
  • GEM: Geography (GEO), Economics, Mathematics (MTC)

All A-Level students also take General Paper and ICT as subsidiary subjects.

Academic Resources

Library

Our well-stocked library contains over 5,000 books, journals, and digital resources to support research and independent learning.

Tutoring

Extra tutoring sessions are available for students who need additional support in specific subjects.

Study Groups

Organized study groups help students collaborate, share knowledge, and prepare for examinations together.

Career Guidance

Our career guidance program helps students make informed decisions about their future academic and career paths.

Our Teaching Staff

Experienced Educators

Our teaching staff consists of highly qualified and experienced educators who are passionate about teaching and dedicated to the success of every student. Many of our teachers hold advanced degrees in their respective fields and regularly participate in professional development programs to stay updated with the latest teaching methodologies.

We maintain a low student-to-teacher ratio to ensure that each student receives the attention and support they need to excel academically. Our teachers serve not only as instructors but also as mentors and role models for our students.
