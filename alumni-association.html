Welcome to the MBSS Alumni Association

The Monsignor Bala Secondary School Alumni Association is a vibrant community of former students who remain connected to their alma mater and to each other. Founded in 2010, our association serves as a bridge between the past, present, and future of MBSS.

We are committed to fostering lifelong relationships, supporting current students, and contributing to the continued growth and excellence of our beloved school.

Our Mission

To maintain strong connections among MBSS alumni, support the school's development, and provide mentorship and opportunities for current students while fostering a spirit of giving back to our community.

Our Vision

To be a dynamic and influential network of MBSS graduates who actively contribute to the advancement of education, community development, and the continued excellence of Monsignor Bala Secondary School.

Benefits of Membership

Networking Opportunities

Connect with fellow alumni across various industries and professions. Build valuable professional and personal relationships.

Exclusive Events

Access to alumni reunions, professional development workshops, social gatherings, and special school events.

Career Support

Job opportunities, career mentorship, professional references, and access to our alumni career network.

Alumni Newsletter

Stay updated with school news, alumni achievements, upcoming events, and opportunities through our quarterly newsletter.

Mentorship Programs

Opportunity to mentor current students or be mentored by senior alumni in your field of interest.

Give Back

Participate in scholarship programs, school development projects, and community service initiatives.
