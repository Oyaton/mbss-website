Alumni Network

Our Alumni Community

The Monsignor Bala Secondary School alumni network consists of graduates who have gone on to make significant contributions in various fields including business, education, healthcare, technology, and public service.

Our alumni remain an integral part of our school community, providing mentorship, career guidance, and support to current students. We take pride in their achievements and the positive impact they continue to make in society.

Alumni Association

Join Our Alumni Association

The MBSS Alumni Association brings together former students to maintain connections, organize reunions, and support the school's development initiatives. Membership is open to all graduates of Monsignor Bala Secondary School.

The association meets quarterly and organizes annual reunions, career fairs, and fundraising events to support scholarships for current students.
